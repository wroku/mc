Meal configurator

This site is designed to help you find an answer to the most important question of the day:

What's for dinner?

You can look through recipes waiting for an inspiration to kick in or search for something specific. Order meals by caloric value, preparation time or price estimation.

Recipes

Got some food laying around and no idea how to combine it into tasty dinner? Add ingredients to a list and search for recipes fitting your needs. Results page will underline items which you still need to acquire. In unlikely case that records do not sufficiently cover combination you are looking for we will also show you some alternatives (partially matching your query). Don't forget to leave a comment with your review or suggestions afterwards!
